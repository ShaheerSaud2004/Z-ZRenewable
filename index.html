New Jersey Solar Incentives

Take advantage of New Jersey's generous incentives to make your switch to solar more affordable than ever.

30% Federal Tax Credit

Receive a tax credit for 30% of your total system cost, including equipment and installation, reducing your federal tax liability.

Successor Solar Incentive (SuSI)

Earn $85 for every megawatt-hour (MWh) your system produces. This provides a steady, long-term return on your investment.

Zero Sales Tax

Your entire solar panel system is exempt from New Jersey's 6.625% sales tax, providing significant upfront savings.

Property Tax Exemption

Your property taxes will not increase, even though a solar installation adds significant value to your home.
